Human Values

Why Human Values?

IAHV addresses the urgent need to rekindle universal human values throughout all sectors of society. We nurture and strengthen human values by working with any organization to ensure that they are acknowledged, honored, encouraged and rewarded.

What does Human Values mean to us?

Following the release of the United Nations Millennium Development goals, IAHV through its founder proposed the Universal Declaration of Human Values, which presents a fresh approach to fostering understanding and harmonious coexistence among different peoples and cultures, necessary to meet these goals.

About IAHV

Today the global IAHV network spans the globe. IAHV conducts projects for the community that promotes the physical health and mental wellbeing of people and raises funds for humanitarian projects and disaster relief aid. Community

We are committed to enhancing the quality of life for all people by building a global community based on the human values that we all share in common, celebrating the diversity of cultural and religious identities.
Join us in this sacred endeavor of reawakening human values throughout the community

Through a multitude of self-awareness, education and outreach programs, IAHV aims to revive human values that transcend religious, ethnic and cultural differences.
These values include a sense of belonging to a common humanity, respect and consideration for all people, compassion and non-violence, a commitment to preserving the natural environment, and a social service ethics.

Our Initiatives

Yes 4 Schools

Breathing Life into Education

As students learn how to manage their stress in a healthy way, they exhibit greater confidence and motivation to succeed in school and make healthy choices when faced with life’s challenges.
Young people face emotional issues such as low self-esteem, depression and anxiety Without a healthy alternative, students often turn to unhealthy strategies to cope with these problems such as smoking, alcohol or drugs.
By offering youth practical tools and life skills to manage stress, YES! for Schools is addressing a critical issue in education today
See more: www.youthempowermentseminar.org

TLEX

Transforming leadership in the corporate world

What truly makes an effective organizational leader are the soft skills: the ability to connect with coworkers and inspire teams, the talent to communicate with such clarity and confidence that drives change, and to remain so positive that they can transform challenges into opportunities.
The TLEX transformational leadership development and corporate wellness programs equip employees with the energy and clarity of mind to achieve personal excellence, make better decisions, and work more effectively on teams.
We believe that high performance is better achieved and sustained when employees are inspired, engaged, and fully present.
See more: www.tlexinstitute.com

Prison Smart

Stress Management and Rehabilitation Training

"Expand your vision and see that inside every culprit, there is a victim crying for help. That person is also a victim of ignorance, small-mindedness and lack of awareness. It's the stress, lack of understanding, lack of broad vision about life, and bad communication that leads to violence in society."

Sri Sri Ravi Shankar, IAHV Founder

The mission of IAHV Prison SMART is to make a life-transforming difference in the lives of all people within the criminal justice system, by teaching skills for reducing stress, healing trauma, and providing practical knowledge of how to handle one's emotions, to help them live their highest potential and contribute to society.
See more: www.prisonsmart.org

Who are we?

Our History

The International Association for Human Values was created in Geneva in 1997 by His Holiness Sri Sri Ravi Shankar as a global platform for humanitarian initiatives that solve problems by uplifting human values.

The Inspiration - Sri Sri Ravi Shankar

It takes a special person to hold such titles as spiritual leader, humanitarian, ambassador of peace and human values, but to transcend his message across different cultures, religious identities and political climates is something the world has not seen in one person for a very long time.
The power of his message and presence has galvanized a volunteer base from over 150 countries working on initiatives to make a stress free and violence free world.
Establishing IAHV in 1997 was the beginning of his major initiatives and addresses emphasizing the need to reinforce human values and recognize humanity as our highest identity.
He has fostered interfaith harmony and called for multi-cultural education as the remedy for fanaticism and to achieve sustainable peace on our planet. Read more: www.srisriravishankar.org

Our Corporate Philosophy

The following values are critical in guiding our thinking and behaviour and how we operate our organisation and influence our decision-making through these ever changing times

  • We value positive and respectful attitudes operating with self-awareness and confidence
  • We value debate and learning to make robust valued decisions
  • We acknowledge the beliefs, views and attitudes of others, while at the same time having clear, concise and rational beliefs of our own
  • In this world where opposites are far too often complementary, we don’t mind being different to others
